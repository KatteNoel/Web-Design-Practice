The Black Eagles are one of the three houses of Garreg Mach Monastery's Officer's Academy. This year's batch of students include Edelgard, Hubert, Ferdinand, Dorothea, Bernadetta, Caspar, Linhardt, and Petra. The Black Eagles are particularly strong in magic, with Hubert's, Dorothea's, and Linhardt's default classes being magic-wielders. Edelgard has a hidden talent for Reason, and Bernadetta is an adept mage as well.
